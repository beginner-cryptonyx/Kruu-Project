ACHIEVEMENTS - Vaccine Jet began as a small company in a bustling Indian town and has now expanded to become a global success. Vaccine Jet now provides over 100,000 immunisations daily at about 7000 centers, breaking its previous record of 90,000 immunisations every day. Thanks to cutting-edge technology and rigorous quality controls, our vaccines are now secure and efficient, as been validated by the WHO.
WHO WE ARE - Team Vaccine Jet has been around for 4 years now, and we have continued to develop and expand our centres and accessibility to our customers. An amazing number of staff look after the management of our websites and social media. When the pandemic COVID -19 began, we realised the importance of vaccines. So we started with the vaccines available in the market at that time, for our neighbourhood. As our business grew, we decided to also give out vaccines for COVID -19.
